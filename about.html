About Mario The Maker

Mario Cruz is an accomplished technologist, entrepreneur, inventor, and musician from Miami, FL. As a kid, I would take things apart and mostly put them back together. Then I started fixing musical instruments and building computers — and I was hooked. Today I work out of Moonlighter FabLab in Miami, creating robots, IoT devices, and interactive installations that bring technology and creativity together.

Professional Background

I serve as CTO of Watsco Ventures, helping HVAC/R entrepreneurs innovate and grow their businesses. With over 19 years of experience in technology leadership, I've held executive roles including CTO positions at Choose Digital and Grass Roots America, plus earlier positions in technology infrastructure and engineering.

Maker Faire Miami

I'm the Executive Producer of Maker Faire Miami, our flagship event designed to celebrate, educate, and inspire our local maker community. In 2019, the Moonlighter team took over production and we've never looked back. Maker Faire Miami is produced by Moonlighter FabLab, a 501(c)3 Non-Profit Organization dedicated to empowering makers with access to the skills, tools, and opportunities to shape a better future.

Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden

I serve as a Technical Advisor for Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden, designing IoT solutions for their programs. I'm proud to support the Growing Beyond Earth initiative — a partnership with NASA that engages students in real space biology research by growing plants in controlled environments and contributing data to help develop sustainable food production for future space missions.

Moonlighter FabLab

I serve on the Board of Directors at Moonlighter FabLab, a non-profit S.T.E.A.M. learning center in Miami. Moonlighter provides access to CNC machines, laser cutters, 3D printers, and more. We mentor students and startups, and are dedicated to empowering makers through hands-on education and resources. If you're in Miami and want to start making, come check us out!
