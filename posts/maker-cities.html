The Future of Work Isn't Robots. It's Makers!

November 5, 2025

There's a prevailing narrative about artificial intelligence and employment—that machines will replace workers. I believe something different: the future belongs to the humans who know how to leverage those technologies.

Introducing Maker Cities

Maker Cities is an NSF-funded program launched by Moonlighter FabLab. The initiative aims to prepare adults for future careers through hands-on training in advanced manufacturing, digital design, and problem-solving.

What You'll Learn

This six-month, studio-based program accepts 20 participants annually and covers:

  • CAD/CAM design
  • 3D printing
  • Laser cutting
  • CNC milling
  • Design thinking
  • Entrepreneurship

Participants collaborate with professional mentors to create civic projects on Miami Beach's Lincoln Road, directly shaping their community.

No Experience Required

The program requires no STEM background or prior experience. Participants receive:

  • $5,000 stipend
  • One-year FabLab residency
  • Professional mentorship
  • Industry certifications

Apply Now

Applications for Cohort 1 (2026) are open. Visit moonlighterfablab.org/maker-cities to apply.

The future isn't about robots taking jobs—it's about makers creating them.
